Our Team

Meet Our Team at Travipedia!

Our team at Travipedia is a passionate group of travel enthusiasts dedicated to crafting unforgettable experiences for our clients. With a wealth of experience in the travel industry and a deep love for exploration, each member brings a unique set of skills and expertise to the table. Get to know the faces behind the scenes who work tirelessly to ensure that every aspect of your journey is meticulously planned and flawlessly executed.

Nitesh Bhat

Founder and CEO

Nitesh is a seasoned traveller, a creative designer. His passion for exploration fuels his travels, which in turn inspire his design and development work. Nitesh's experiences on the road allow him to bring a unique perspective to both his creative and business endeavors. As the CEO of Travipedia, he leverages his combined talents to develop innovative products and services that empower fellow travelers to plan and experience unforgettable adventures.

Harsh Kumar

Co-Founder

As a Co-founder, Harsh brings a complementary skillset to the table, perfectly balancing Nitesh's creative vision. With his expertise in development and business strategy and a traveler himself, Harsh understands the needs and desires of Travipedia's user base on a personal level. Harsh ensures Travipedia's innovative ideas translate into a successful and sustainable business shaping it into a valuable resource for the travel community.

Nikhil Utwal

Chief Marketing Officer

Nikhil, Travipedia's Chief Marketing Officer (CMO), rounds out the leadership team with his strategic marketing expertise. A keen traveler Nikhil leverages his own travel experiences to craft compelling narratives that resonate with Travipedia's target audience. His understanding of the travel industry and his ability to connect with potential users play a vital role in driving brand awareness and user acquisition for Travipedia.

Ankit Kumar

Chief Technical Officer

Ankit, Travipedia's Chief Technical Officer (CTO), brings the technical muscle to the table, ensuring the vision and direction translate into a flawlessly functioning platform. His expertise in software development keeps Travipedia at the forefront of travel technology. Ankit's passion for travel fuels his commitment to building a user-friendly and innovative platform that empowers travelers to explore the world seamlessly.
